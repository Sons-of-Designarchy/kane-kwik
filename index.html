ABOUT

Kane was born in Palma de Mallorca (Spain) and started his cinematography career at an early age. He studied Direction of Photography at the CECC (Barcelona), although he soon discovered his vocation for film directing. Working as a director Kane preserves innovation and aesthetic sense to the extreme. This expertise combined with his story telling skills constitutes the strong points of his imagery.

As an international director he has worked in numerous countries, such as Spain, Mexico, India, Italy, France, Panama, Argentina.
