ABOUT

Kane was born in Palma de Mallorca (Spain) and started his cinematography career at an early age. He studied Direction of Photography at the CECC (Barcelona), although he soon discovered his vocation for film directing. Working as a director Kane preserves innovation and aesthetic sense to the extreme. This expertise combined with his story telling skills constitutes the strong points of his imagery.

As an international director he has worked in numerous countries, such as Spain, Mexico, India, Italy, France, Panama, Argentina. He has received several international awards: Cannes Lions 2019 (Silver, Bronze), "Best Music Video“ at the Lower East Side Film Festival (NYC), “Best Director” at the Austin Music Video Festival (Austin) and “Mejor Director” (Best Director) at the Pantalla de Cristal Festival (Mexico), “Best Director” at El Ojo de Iberoamérica 2019, among others.

When it comes to creating, Kane is a perfectionist and quickly understands everyone’s needs, whereas on set he is relaxed and sociable. He always finds a way of getting the best from everybody, both actors and crew, by motivating and involving the whole team in the shootings.
